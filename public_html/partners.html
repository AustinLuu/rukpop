Partner With Us

Why Parnter with RU K-POP?

Collaborating with RU K-POP will create prime-marketing opportunities for you as it provides you with the ability to reach a diverse group of more than 1,500 university and college students (mostly those attending Ryerson University) in the heart of downtown Toronto. By partnering with us, your organization will be associated with one of Toronto’s largest student groups dedicated to the promotion and celebration of the Korean entertainment industry.

While our focus is on Korean entertainment, thanks to the “Hallyu Wave”, our audience has become diverse and expanded over the past couple of years. By partnering with RU K-POP, not only will you be demographically diversifying your market and creating new channels for word-of-mouth and viral online marketing, but you will also be helping students achieve an enhanced lifestyle by providing them with new and unique experiences. This will leave them with a lasting impression of your organization – one which they will carry with them for many years to come, potentially increasing your chances of developing a loyal customer base early on.

Benefits of Partnering With Us

  • Recognition at our formal events, including honourable mentions at our orientations, games nights, movie nights, language workshops, and performance nights
  • Brand placement in our marketing campaigns (social media, newsletters, posters & flyers, RU K-POP merchandise)
  • Promotional posts on our social media sites/apps (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat)
  • We are also very much open to specific requests made by your organization! E-mail us now at rukpop@ryerson.ca!
