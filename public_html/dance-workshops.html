Dance Workshops

Whether it is for fitness, health, educational purposes, or simply enjoyment, our monthly dance workshops provide a space for like-minded individuals to get together and create valuable memories. Every month, we promote a short-term theme unique to each workshop - hinting at which kinds of dances will be taught (summer bops, solo artists, throwbacks, etc.). Our long-term and ongoing concept is that of a “commUNITY”, because where there is a positive group environment, growth & learning ultimately follows & prospers.

Note: Our dance workshops are instructed by RU K-POP’s Dance Team and are generally taught to appeal to dancers of all levels of experience, unless stated otherwise.
